Cheese Pizza Recipe

Description:

A classic cheese pizza consists of pizza dough, tomato sauce, and shredded mozzarella cheese, often topped with parmesan, herbs, and olive oil, baked until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly. It can be customized with varied cheeses like provolone, fontina, or cheddar.

Ingredients:

  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pizza dough

Steps

  1. Steep oil with garlic
  2. Stretch the dough onto a pizza pan
  3. Spread the garlic infused oil over the dough
  4. Sprinkle with parmesan then mozzarella, leaving a border around the crust
  5. Bake for 8 minutes in a hot 250°C / 480°F (230°C fan) oven, rotating the tray halfway, until you have golden spots on the cheese.
  6. Cut and eat!
